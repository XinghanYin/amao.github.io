prototyping

The ideation process lets projects grow rapidly – anything could change along the way, with each step based solely on the previous developmental version. And by focusing on constant adjustment and ongoing modification, a shift towards user involvement and feedback has occurred – the interaction of ideas wins out over functional details. Plus prototyping is cheaper and saves time, and that’s why it perfectly suits the open-ended search for innovation.
