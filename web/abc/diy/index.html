DIY innovation

What’s the next phase in the democratisation of technology? Since it always seems to bubble up from the unlikeliest of rabbit holes, we’re placing our bets on the younger generations’ ability to learn coding. Then there’s creative engagement through digital enhancements to public space – can't forget about that. Homespun tech will no doubt keep everyone guessing: lo-fi interventions, iOS jailbreaking and dual SIM (where phones are hacked to capitalise on cheaper tariffs from different carriers) are only the beginning.
