voice control

As the remote control of the future begins to inspire the next flood of consumer goods, the gradual disappearance of analogue interfaces (buttons, dials, switches) will be interesting to watch. Mobile apps are already leading the way, with hands-free commands being woven into the basic design of everything, from cars to appliances. The digital assistants are awaiting our beck and call, ready to serve. Thankfully voice recognition software can help make sure the thieves and impostors won’t get the upper hand.
