crowdsourcing

We now find ourselves in a modern-day gift economy, where sharing ideas has the potential to transform any project into something stronger and more successful. But this doesn’t mean open innovation and problem solving are free – intellect, man hours and funding can’t be forgotten. And for any co-created activity to truly work, group objectives must equal personal intentions. This progressive approach has also encouraged crowdfunding, where donors, sponsors and investors pool resources through online contributions.

‘Outsourcing the crowd’ has evolved into well-known funding platforms like Kickstarter, which help thousands of people to get their projects off the ground.
