telematics

We are being surveilled – in our cars, at the gym and while we sleep. But the information collected by our personal devices won’t necessarily be used in sinister ways – or will it? Digital sensors can now be embedded within most machines to monitor their performance. So, as the thinking goes, why not apply this to the human body as well? Telematics, or the wireless data shared through connected systems, illuminates a new path towards greater rewards and the end of wilful ignorance.
