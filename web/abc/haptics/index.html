haptics

Our sense of touch brings us into direct contact with the external world, and haptic technology seeks to incorporate this tactile feedback into our everyday interactions with machines. With vibrations, pressure and movement, simulation brings stimulation, where virtual objects spring to life through non-verbal communication.

Royal College of Art graduate Gabriele Meldaikyte explored the different unique gestures we all use when touching our phones. She then attempted to recreate them as physical interfaces.
