internet of things

Connecting our existing infrastructure of machines to the internet sounds a bit scary, but the results are as exciting as they are potentially liberating. IoT means that messages can be triggered, whenever and however we want, based on predefined conditions and actions that we control. This brave new world of digital connectivity can be configured for all types of situations and environments, starting with the domestic space.
