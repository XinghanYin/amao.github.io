nomophobia

Being without a phone is terrifying. We feel naked and helpless without it. Our mobile must always be within reach, 24 hours a day, whether we need it or not. But there's an even darker side: the obsessive behaviour that comes with phone-induced anxiety. This compulsion – constantly checking for emails, missed calls and new messages – can be pronounced, exhaustive and all consuming. Is there an app for that?
