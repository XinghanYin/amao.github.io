hot spot

Wouldn’t it be great if everyone could access the internet, anywhere and entirely for free? The answer is ‘yes’ and that’s why hot spots are the future of broadband. And while open public networks have increased in recent years, the free model is still the exception rather than the rule. But with bandwidth increasing, there’s even more reason to create access on the local level, regardless of the security issues and financial constraints.

SHARE
Karma is a small portable Wi-Fi hotspot that allows users to share and sell internet connectivity to others. The more people you share it with, the cheaper your own rates become.
