You adore your e-reader or don’t even own one because you prefer the feel of paper. Either way, fair enough. But the epoch of the ebook is upon us, so get ready – things are moving to mobile, and fast. The fact that you're reading this at all suggests that you often peruse a newspaper or magazine on a portable device. But what about tackling an entire novel or textbook on a feature phone? For both the developing and the developed world, mobile reading will fundamentally change the way society approaches literacy, learning and the distribution of written knowledge. And large publishers must do more to adapt – in book design, distribution and interactivity – or risk losing everything during the new wave of digital innovation.
